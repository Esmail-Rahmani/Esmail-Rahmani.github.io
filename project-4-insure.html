Project Overview

INSURE AID (Gandharvs Insure Aid (OPC) Pvt. Ltd.) is a Startup India recognised Insurance management and grievance redressal tech platform for the insurance policyholders who are unaware about the claim process, victims of insurance related fraud, and has unclaimed amount. At Insure Aid we help you with a Policy Wallet, Nominee assistance program, Track and claim of unclaimed amount, and claim settlement service with a grievance redressal platform. Insure Aid stop your insurance benefits from slipping into the insurance unclaimed funds and helps you ensure that all your insurance benefits reach your family when you are not there.
